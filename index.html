WELCOMETO OUR COMPANY

Introduction to Svnty Degree

Svnty Degree is a dynamic venture founded by Rachna Dham, with Vivekanand Tiwari as the cofounder. Rachna brings over 20 years of exceptional experience in the industry. She began her career under the guidance of a top architect in Delhi, later moving to TATA AIG as a Project Manager, where she gained valuable experience for three years. Following this, she embarked on a freelance journey and then founded Squirrel Design House. Squirrel Design House, co-founded with Vivekananda Tiwari, a marketing specialist, successfully operated the Company with a strong track record of delivering turnkey projects for prestigious clients like Dr. Lal Path Labs, SBI, Bank of Baroda, and leading PSUs such as Bharat Petroleum, Indian Oil Corporation Ltd., International Center for Automotive Technology. She also done turnkey auditorium projects with MJP Rohailkhand University and Gurugram University. Rachna and Vivekanand joined hands and now spearheaded the establishment of RDVT under Svnty Degree. This venture brings together exceptional ideas, precise timelines, and collaborations with top Indian and international brands, ensuring unparalleled success in every project.
